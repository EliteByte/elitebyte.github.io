About

Autobiography in a time-line format. Learn all of my secrets.*

  • 2001-2012

    My Humble Upbringing

    Born January 12, 2001 in Los Angeles, California. I was raised by a mixture of family members but was and always will be focused on my education. Found out that I was a scholar during fourth grade and challenged myself left and right.

  • 2013-2015

    My Passion is Born

    During this period I was given my first and only laptop, a Macbook Air (Calm down Apple loathers). At first I used it to play one of my favorite games, Minecraft, which then lead me to the interest in Minecraft development; also known as, Java development. I used these middle schol years to self-teach myself Java, followed by introducing myself to other languages like LUA, and JavaScript.

  • 2016

    Coming-of-Age

    Faced by a multitude of decisions that involved my education I began going to MSTMA in Boyle Heights. Joined a Robotics team, LAStreetbots #4964. Had tons of fun with robotics, learned about the basics of electrical engineering and the integration with microprocessing in order to manipulate motors, pneumatics, and lots of LED's!

  • Current

    Hopeful Adventure

    Currently hoping to work on iOS apps during the summer on top of summer school. The rest is yet to be lived and written....
